# CPE Credit Rules

## Overview
Continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits are required for renewal. Credits are tracked by category and reporting period.

## Categories
- Core audit practice
- Regulatory updates
- Ethics and professional conduct

## Minimum Thresholds
- Total credits per period: 40
- Ethics credits minimum: 4
- Regulatory credits minimum: 6

## Validation
- Each activity must include provider name, completion date, and credit value.
- Activities are subject to audit and must retain supporting documentation for 3 years.
