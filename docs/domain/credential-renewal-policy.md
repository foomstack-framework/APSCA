# Credential Renewal Policy

## Scope
Applies to all credentialed auditors who must renew on a fixed cadence.

## Renewal Window
- Standard renewal window opens 90 days before expiration.
- Grace period extends 30 days after expiration with additional attestations.

## Attestations
- Code of ethics compliance
- Continued professional practice confirmation
- Disclosure of any disciplinary actions

## Fees
- Standard renewal fee applies within the normal window.
- Late penalties apply during the grace period.

## Exceptions
Exceptions require documented approval and must be logged for audit review.
