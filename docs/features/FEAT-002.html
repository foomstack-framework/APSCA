FEAT-002: Credential Renewal Portal

Status: Confirmed

Purpose

Enable auditors to renew credentials, submit attestations, and pay renewal fees

Business Value

Reduces manual renewal processing, improves compliance visibility, and gives auditors a self-service renewal experience

In Scope

  • Renewal eligibility checks
  • Attestation submission and storage
  • Renewal fee invoicing
  • Renewal status dashboard for auditors

Out of Scope

  • Credential issuance and revocation
  • External audit scheduling
  • Accounting reconciliation
